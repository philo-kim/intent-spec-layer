# Code Of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant principles in a lightweight form.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Critique ideas, claims, and evidence rather than people.
- Prefer reproducible examples over broad assertions.
- Assume contributors may come from different domains and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Repeated off-topic promotion.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately misleading experiments or citations.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block contributors who
repeatedly violate these expectations.

Reference: <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/>
